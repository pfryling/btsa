Biomedical Technology Student Association

About

The Biomedical Technology Student Association (BTSA) is the largest biotechnology club at San Diego State University. With informative guest speakers, tours of local industry, and a strong alumni network the BTSA provides a unique opportunity for students at SDSU.

The composition of our club is comprised of both undergraduate and graduate students, and members majoring in biology, chemistry, engineering, business, computer science, bioinformatics and more!
