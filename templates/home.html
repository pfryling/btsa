Biomedical Technology Student Association

About

We are a biotechnology club at San Diego State University. With awesome guest speakers, tours of local industry, and a strong alumni network the BTSA provides a unique opportunity for students at SDSU.

The composition of our club is very diverse with both undergraduate and graduate students, and members majoring in computer science, biology, bio-engineering, chemistry, bioinformatics and more.

The BTSA is the greatest thing since sliced bread.

Stanley Maloy - Dean of the College of Sciences

The BTSA is a great way to learn about available internships, network with employers, and discover exciting career opportunities in specialized fields you may not have previously considered.

Paul Fryling - BTSA co-founder
