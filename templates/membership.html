BTSA Membership


Official membership is $10 per semester or $15 per academic year. The dues are used to fund events, club merchandise, and general activities throughout the school year.

If you cannot make the meeting times, but would still like to join the club. You are still eligible to become a member by attending 5 of our events and paying dues. Events will appear on our calendar so make sure you check it out, and there will be plenty of opportunities for you to come join a club event!
