Becoming a Corperate Sponsor

Make a positive impact on students lives by supporting our rapidly growing non-profit organization. There are a variety of partnership options from becoming a patron, to seeking an advisory position and joining the SDSU Biomedical Technology Student Association Board of Advisors.

  • Become a Patron ($100)

    • As a Patron of the BTSA, you will be listed on our website and recognized for your support.

  • Sponsor an Event ($250 - 500)

    • When sponsoring an Event, you and your business will be acknowledged throughout the event, shared on our Facebook page, and also listed on our website.

  • Sponsor a Speaker Series ($1000)

    • Similarly to sponsoring an individual event, when sponsoring a Speaker Series, you and your business will be acknowledged throughout the events, thanked on our Facebook page, and also highly regarded on our website.

  • Become an Advisor ($500)

    • The purpose of the advisory board is assisting the Entrepreneur Society Executive Board with access to resources, mentorship, and strategic guidance for a sustainable and effective organization. Contact {{clubSettings.president.name}}, the BTSA President to receive more information.
